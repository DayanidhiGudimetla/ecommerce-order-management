# 📊 Key Business Insights

## Revenue Findings
- Electronics leads all categories in gross revenue
- Discounts above 10% reduce net margins significantly
- Q4 (Oct–Dec) generates the highest quarterly sales

## Customer Behavior
- ~40% of revenue comes from repeat customers
- Top 3 customers contribute 30%+ of total revenue
- Maharashtra and Kerala are top states by revenue

## Product Performance
- Mobile phones and laptops are highest revenue items
- Low-cost items (books, bottles) have high unit sales but low revenue
- Sony and Samsung electronics have zero return complaints in data

## Operational Insights
- Express shipping correlates with higher average order values
- Standard shipping dominates in volume (65% of orders)

## Recommendations
1. Focus retention campaigns on top 5 customers
2. Bundle low-value items to increase average order value
3. Run targeted promotions in high-performing states
4. Limit discounts to max 5% to protect margins
